Rock, Paper, Scissors

This is a basic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors that was completed in C#. I attempted this in my early stages of learning C# essentially to test my understanding of the language at that point. This program is a console application, but fully functioning.

Essentially, this quick project helped develop my understanding for switch cases and how they are used in C#.

The program will ask you for a score limit, and then you will pick between rock, paper and scissors. The computer's pick will be determined completely at random, and score will be displayed after every round. First to reach the score limit wins!
